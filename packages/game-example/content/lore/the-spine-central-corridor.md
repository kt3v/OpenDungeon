# The Spine Central Corridor

The Spine is the long emergency-lit corridor-trunk that runs through the ship and links the major sections together. It is one of the main circulation routes aboard Sprint.

Vyr marauders and wild Sylph are often encountered here. Because many key areas branch from the Spine, it becomes a natural place for patrols, ambushes, and forced detours.

Velocity often seals corridor sections or changes routes through environmental controls, turning straightforward movement into a maze.
