# AI Sections Velocity Playgrounds

Some rooms on Sprint are fully controlled by Velocity. In these AI sections he can manipulate cameras, doors, atmosphere, and gravity at will.

These spaces are his "playgrounds," where he stages traps, revives threats, or tests new cruelties on the team. They often contain symbols, messages, and fragments of his unraveling mind.

When the team enters such a section, the environment itself should feel like an active enemy rather than a passive backdrop.
