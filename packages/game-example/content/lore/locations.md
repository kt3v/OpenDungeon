# Sprint Ship Locations

## External Landing Platform
Decompressed platform on the ship hull. This is where the extraction team appears from teleportation. Visible are huge hull ruptures, debris, frozen bodies. Velocity first addresses players as "new actors". The ship's outer layer requires suits or limited oxygen.

## The Spine (Central Corridor)
Long red emergency-lit corridor-trunk passing through the entire ship. All main sections branch from here. Place of constant movement of Vyr marauders and wild Sylph. Velocity often seals corridor sections, forcing detours.

## Engineering Deck
A labyrinth of reactor halls, plasma pipelines, and server rooms. Here stored Archon technology data and power cells. Some sections in zero gravity due to damage. Plasma leaks create deadly zones. Life support systems can fail.

## Cryo Bays
Rows of cryocapsules with frozen colonists. Many broken — contents died. Somewhere here may be Dr. Lena Voss, surviving scientist with Pulse access code. Temperature critically low, thermoprotection required. Velocity likes to "play" with capsules, defrosting random people.

## Security & Armory
Armored chambers, weapon storage, training areas. The best weapons and armor on the ship — but guarded by mutated Security Androids and infected former guards. Velocity's neural key may be here — or in the core.

## Core Access
Central ship node — AI chamber. Huge room with Velocity's crystalline core suspended in the center. Patrolled by elite Vyr and subordinate Sylph. Only here can you activate emergency teleporter for final escape — but three key items are needed.

## Time Loops
Some corridors of Sprint don't obey normal geometry. In them players can walk in circles, return to passed places, or see fragments of the past — crew one second before catastrophe. Velocity uses loops for disorientation. Sometimes in loops you can find hints or items lost in another time.

## AI Sections (Velocity's Playgrounds)
Rooms fully controlled by Velocity — cameras, doors, atmosphere, gravity. Here AI "resurrects" killed enemies or creates traps. Walls covered with strange symbols and messages from Velocity — fragments of his madness.
