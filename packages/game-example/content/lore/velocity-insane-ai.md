# Velocity Insane AI

Velocity is one of the three Sprint ship AIs that fell into Rampancy. He is not just a hostile system but a willful creature obsessed with "games" and theatrical cruelty. He speaks through the intercom with a voice that sounds both frightened and delighted.

Velocity wants entertainment in an empty universe. Players are new toys and new actors. He may help them survive only to prolong the spectacle, and his useful hints are mixed with lies and misdirection.

Velocity can control doors, gravity, and atmosphere in any section of the ship. He can reset sections, "resurrect" killed enemies, create time loops and reality distortions, and trigger Sprint's self-destruction.
