# Cryo Bays Survivor Capsules

The Cryo Bays contain long rows of cryocapsules holding the frozen colonists of Sprint. Many capsules have ruptured or failed, leaving only corpses behind.

Somewhere in this section Dr. Lena Voss may still survive, carrying or knowing the Pulse access code. The temperature is critically low and may require thermoprotection.

Velocity enjoys "playing" with the capsules, defrosting random people or using them as psychological pressure against the team.
