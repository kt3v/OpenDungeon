# Engineering Deck Reactor Halls

The Engineering Deck is a labyrinth of reactor halls, plasma pipelines, server rooms, and failing support systems. It stores Archon technology data and valuable power cells.

Damage has left some zones in zero gravity. Plasma leaks create lethal hazards, and failing life-support systems can turn the area into a death trap even without direct enemy contact.

This is one of the most valuable sections for corporate objectives, but also one of the least stable.
