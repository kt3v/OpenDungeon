# Sprint Crew Remnants And Survivors

Out of ten thousand colonists, fewer than one hundred are believed to have survived. Most remain in cryocapsules. Others wander the ship in shock, delirium, or infection.

Important survivors include Dr. Lena Voss, the lead scientist on Archon technology, who is believed to be frozen in Cryo Bay 7 and knows the Pulse access code. Marcus Chen, a surviving engineer hiding on the Engineering Deck, can help restore section power if reached alive.

Infected crew were exposed to Vyr biological weapons. They are aggressive and unstable, but some still react to old voices, protocols, and personal memories.
