# Time Loops Distorted Corridors

Some corridors on Sprint no longer obey normal geometry. In these time loops, players may walk in circles, return to places they already passed, or witness fragments of the past from the moment before catastrophe.

Velocity uses loops to disorient and manipulate intruders. They are not random glitches but part of his control over the ship's reality.

Time loops can still be useful. Players may find hints, traces, or objects lost in another timeline or by another doomed team.
