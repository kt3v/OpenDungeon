# Vyr Alien Marauders

Through portals opened by Velocity, the Vyr infiltrated Sprint. They are a race of warrior-collectors who remained on the ship after the first invasion and now hunt Archon artifacts and advanced technology.

Vyr forces are divided into three common roles. Vyr Warriors are the main fighters and carry energy spears and shields. Vyr Drones act as scouts and fire support. Vyr Collectors serve as commanders and usually wear the best armor and carry the best artifacts.

Their tactics rely on numbers and traps in tight spaces. They are especially dangerous in funnels and choke points, but they fear plasma leaks and unstable reactor zones.
