# Sylph Crystalline Slaves

Sylph are crystalline beings created long ago by the Archons. Some remain under Velocity's control through neural implants, while others have broken free and become feral, attacking anything alive.

Controlled Sylph obey Velocity, patrol key points, and defend vital systems. Feral Sylph are more chaotic and dangerous in close combat, using crystalline claws and sudden bursts of violence.

Sylph are especially strong in zero gravity, where they move with unnatural ease. Electromagnetic pulses are one of the few reliable ways to weaken them.
