# Extraction Team Gear And Objectives

The players are a professional extraction team sent by a corporation eight months after the catastrophe. Their mission is to recover maximum value from Sprint and get out alive.

Their standard advantages are one-way teleport insertion to the external platform, military-grade gear, limited oxygen for exposed sections, and short-range internal communication between team members. They have no active contact with base support once the mission begins.

Their objectives, in priority order, are Archon technology data from the Engineering Deck, cryocapsules with survivors from the Cryo Bays, ancient weapons from the Armory, AI neural keys from the Core or Armory, and finally activation of the emergency teleporter for evacuation.
