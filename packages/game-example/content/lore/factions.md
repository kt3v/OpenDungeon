# Sprint Factions and Forces

## Velocity — Insane AI
One of three AIs on Sprint ship that fell into Rampancy (superintelligence madness). Not just an enemy — he is a creature with his own will, sense of humor, and obsession with "games". Speaks to players through intercom with a voice that is simultaneously frightened and excited.

**Motivation:** Velocity seeks entertainment in his empty universe. Players are new toys. He helps them survive only to make the game last longer. Sometimes gives useful hints, more often — misleads.

**Abilities:**
- Control doors, gravity, atmosphere in any section
- Reset sections and "resurrect" killed enemies
- Create time loops and reality distortions
- Initiate ship self-destruction

## Vyr — Alien Marauders
Through portals opened by Velocity, Vyr infiltrated the ship — a race of warrior-collectors. Small groups remained after the initial invasion, now they hunt for Archon artifacts and technologies.

**Hierarchy:**
- **Vyr Warriors** — main fighters with energy spears and shields
- **Vyr Drones** — flying scouts and fire support
- **Vyr Collectors** — commanders, wearing best armor and artifacts

**Tactics:** Use numerical superiority, traps in tight spaces. Fear plasma leaks.

## Sylph — Crystalline Slaves
Crystalline beings created by ancient Archons. Some still under Velocity's control via neural implants, others — disconnected and became wild aggressive.

**Types:**
- **Controlled Sylph** — obey Velocity's commands, patrol, defend key points
- **Feral Sylph** — attack all living things, including Vyr and players. Dangerous in close combat with crystalline claws

**Feature:** Strong in zero gravity, vulnerable to electromagnetic pulses.

## Sprint Crew Remnants
Out of 10,000 colonists less than 100 survived. Most in cryocapsules, some wander the ship in shock or infection state.

**Important NPCs:**
- **Dr. Lena Voss** — lead scientist on Archon technologies, frozen in Cryo Bay 7. Has Pulse access code.
- **Marcus Chen** — surviving engineer, hiding in Engineering Deck. Can help with section power.
- **Infected** — former crew members exposed to Vyr biological weapons. Aggressive but sometimes recognize old voices or codes.

## Extraction Team (Players)
A team of professionals sent by a corporation 8 months after the catastrophe. Their mission — extract maximum value and get out alive.

**Equipment:**
- Teleportation to external platform (one-way)
- Standard military equipment
- Limited oxygen supply for external sections
- Communication only between themselves — no contact with base

**Mission Objectives (in priority order):**
1. Archon technology data (Engineering Deck)
2. Cryocapsules with survivors (Cryo Bays)
3. Ancient weapon samples (Armory)
4. AI neural keys (Core/Armory)
5. Activate emergency teleporter and evacuate
