# Factions and Powers

## The Covenant of Ashes
A loose alliance of city-states that emerged after the Fall of the Old Empire. They maintain order through a network of scouts and inquisitors. Practical, not idealistic.

## The Unseen College
Magic users who operate in secret. They believe magic is too dangerous for public use. Their agents often intervene when magical threats emerge — but their definition of "threat" is broad.

## The Wardens of the Wood
Rangers and druids who protect the wilderness from encroachment. They have little patience for civilization but are not hostile — unless you bring an axe.

## The Ironbound
Mercenaries who follow a strict code of contract. Once hired, they cannot break their word. They are expensive but reliable.
