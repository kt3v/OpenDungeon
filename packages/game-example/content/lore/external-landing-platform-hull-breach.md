# External Landing Platform Hull Breach

The external landing platform is a decompressed structure on Sprint's hull. This is where the extraction team materializes after teleport insertion.

The platform is surrounded by hull ruptures, drifting debris, and frozen bodies. It is one of the first places where Velocity addresses the team as "new actors."

The outer layer of the ship requires suits or other oxygen protection. Exposure here should immediately reinforce the danger of vacuum and limited resources.
