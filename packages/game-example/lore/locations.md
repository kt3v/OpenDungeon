# Notable Locations

## The Shattered Spires
Ancient towers that pierce the clouds, remnants of a forgotten civilization. No one knows who built them or why they fell. Occasionally, strange lights flicker in their upper windows.

## The Mournwood
A vast forest where the trees grow in spirals. Travelers report hearing whispers on the wind. The local villagers refuse to enter after dark.

## The Sunken Citadel
Once a fortress of the Old Empire, now half-swallowed by a marsh. Treasure hunters sometimes return with artifacts. More often, they don't return at all.

## The Hollow Mountains
Dwarven holds carved into living stone. The dwarves are welcoming to those who bring trade goods and discretion. They speak little of what lies deeper in the mountain.
